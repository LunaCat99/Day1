Everett Community College - GRAPH 130


How many quarters have you attended EvCC?

This is my 6th quater at EvCC. I started Fall Qtr of 2022.


What degree/certificate program are you pursuing?

I am currently working on a transfer degree in both studio arts and graphic design. My hope is to transfer to Western Washington to continue pursuing both careers.


Why did you choose this degree/certificate?

I was originally going to school for psychology and sociology but due to life circumstances I had to quit school. Then in 2020 when I was getting ready to start again the pandemic happened. Because of that I had a lot of time to think if I really wanted to go to school for that again or something else. I had always wanted to study art as a kid but coming from a poor and immigrant background, I had a lot of pressure to choose something realistic so I wouldn't be wasting my families money. But the pandemic makes you realize just how quickly we can disappear from this world and I didn't want to have spent whatever life I had doing something I didn't love. So, I chose to come back to study art. And I'm glad I did.


What artist/designer inspires you? Why?

I don't have particular artists or designers that I follow. I usually search and keep up on styles that I'm particularly fond of such as kawaii designs or vintage lettering. Right now I have been following "Janey Illustrations" on TikTok. She is a printmaker and art teacher who works out of a makeshift studio in her living room.


What is your favorite color?

My favorite color is anything pink or purple really, particularly a nice mauve color.
